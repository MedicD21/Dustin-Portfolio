Every programmer's first program prints "Hello, world" — so it felt
right to start this site the same way.

I'm at the very beginning of a software engineering degree, and I
wanted a place to put things as I go: projects from coursework,
experiments that go somewhere (or don't), and short notes about what
I'm figuring out.

Writing about what you're learning is supposed to be one of the best
ways to actually learn it. We'll see. If you're reading this — the
notes here are me thinking out loud, not me claiming expertise.

More soon.
